Equity & Ownership Structure

50/50 Partnership

MSTRPAY: 50% equity interest

Faulu Microfinance Bank: 50% equity interest

Entity: MSTRCash Kenya Ltd

Strategic Insight

Equal ownership ensures aligned incentives and shared strategic direction for long-term success.

Capital Contribution & Financing

Initial Capitalization

MSTRPAY provides €25,000 – €50,000 for:

  • Incorporation and legal setup
  • Launch marketing campaigns
  • Agent onboarding programs
  • Initial operational expenses

Growth Incentives

Faulu receives €3 in MSTRpay shares per verified active user onboarded

MSTRPAY matches Faulu's Tier 1 and Tier 2 equity capital in MSTRPAY shares

Customer-Linked Equity Model

Growth-Based Capital Reinforcement

For each new verified customer:

  • Faulu contributes €0.50 in additional paid-in equity
  • MSTRPAY contributes €0.50 in additional paid-in equity
  • Total: €1.00 per new customer to strengthen capital base

Ownership remains 50/50. Customer count certified quarterly by auditors.

Strategic Insight

Customer-linked equity ensures capital scales with growth, maintaining financial stability.

Profit Sharing & Intellectual Property

Revenue Distribution

Profits distributed 50/50 based on equity ownership

Subject to operational performance and agreed KPIs

IP & Ecosystem
