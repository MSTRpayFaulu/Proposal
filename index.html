Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a 50/50 joint venture to establish MSTRCash Kenya Ltd, a digital banking platform leveraging Faulu's regulatory expertise and MSTRpay's technological ecosystem.

Our Mission

Create Kenya's most integrated digital financial ecosystem combining banking, lifestyle services, and cross-border capabilities.

Our Vision

Serve the next generation of customers with earning opportunities, entertainment, and global connectivity beyond traditional banking.

Strategic Insight

This JV isn't about replacing Faulu's strengths but enhancing them with digital capabilities to capture new customer segments and revenue streams without massive internal CAPEX.

Why Faulu Microfinance Bank?

Regulatory Excellence

CBK-licensed DTM with proven compliance framework and 30+ years market presence.

Customer Base

102,371+ existing clients providing immediate scale and trust foundation.

Financial Strength

CAR: 15% | PAR30: 3% | OSS: 120%
Exceeding all regulatory benchmarks.

Infrastructure

Existing branches, agent network, and digital infrastructure (DigiCash app, USSD).

Strategic Insight

Faulu's existing compliance framework and customer trust allow us to focus resources on growth and innovation rather than building regulatory foundations from scratch.

LOI Clause 3: Equity & Ownership

50/50 Ownership Structure

MSTRPAY: 50% equity interest

Faulu Microfinance Bank: 50% equity interest

Entity: MSTRCash Kenya Ltd

Strategic Insight

True partnership model ensures aligned incentives. Both parties have equal stake in success and equal say in strategic direction, preventing any majority-minority conflicts.

LOI Clause 4: Capital Contribution

Initial Capitalization

MSTRPAY shall allocate €25,000 – €50,000 initial working capital for:

  • Incorporation and legal setup
  • Launch marketing campaigns
  • Agent onboarding programs
  • Initial operational expenses

Equity Matching

MSTRPAY shall match Faulu's Tier 1 and Tier 2 equity capital in MSTRPAY shares.

Client Incentive

Faulu receives €3 in MSTRpay shares per verified active user onboarded through the JV.

LOI Clause 5: Customer-Linked Equity

Growth-Based Capital Reinforcement

For each new verified customer registered by MSTRCash Kenya Ltd:

  • Faulu contributes €0.50 in additional paid-in equity
  • MSTRPAY contributes €0.50 in additional paid-in equity
  • Total: €1.00 per new customer to strengthen capital base

Ownership remains 50/50 at all times. Customer count certified quarterly by company auditors.

Strategic Insight

This innovative model ensures the JV's capital grows proportionally with its customer base, maintaining financial stability while scaling operations.

LOI Clause 6: Profit Sharing & IP

Profit Distribution

Profits distributed 50/50 in accordance with equity ownership

Subject to operational performance and agreed KPIs

Intellectual Property

MSTRPAY retains ownership of mobile application, platform infrastructure, data, and IP rights

All customers become global MSTRPAY users with access to ecosystem services

Strategic Insight

Faulu gains access to MSTRpay's entire ecosystem (MSTRearn, MSTRtv, cross-border rails) without the development cost or time investment.

LOI Clause 7: Office Space Provision

Infrastructure Commitment

Faulu shall provide, at its own cost:

  • Suitable and fully equipped office space within its principal place of business
  • Space for four (4) designated MSTRPAY employees
  • Properly maintained and conducive to professional work
  • For the entire duration of the Agreement

Strategic Insight

Co-location of teams ensures seamless collaboration, faster decision-making, and integration of MSTRpay's expertise with Faulu's market knowledge.

LOI Clause 8: Future Expansion

Strategic Growth Pathway

The Parties recognize the potential to extend scope by:

  • Applying for a Mobile Money Operator (MRO) licence
  • Establishing MSTRBank Kenya Ltd with a licensed Class A commercial bank
  • Following the successful model demonstrated in Burundi

MRO Licence

Enables full mobile money operations, competitive with major players

Class A Banking

Expansion into full commercial banking services and products

LOI Clause 9: Binding Effect

Commitment Framework

This LOI is intended as a binding framework regarding:

  • Equity structure and ownership
  • Capital commitments
  • Profit-sharing principles
  • Customer-linked equity top-up obligations

Detailed governance, operational management, and regulatory approvals to be specified in the final Joint Venture Agreement (JVA).

LOI Clause 2A: Financial Prerequisite

Faulu's Verified Financial Standing

As prerequisite to the JV establishment, the Parties acknowledge:

  • Clients: 102,371
  • Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR): 15% (exceeds DTM minimum requirement)
